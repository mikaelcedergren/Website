About

When it comes to getting things done, I'm like a Swiss Army knife with a paintbrush. With 25 years of experience in Art Direction, Motion Graphics, and Graphic Design, I've been lucky enough to spend my days (and sometimes nights) bringing art to life through motion graphics, graphic design, and art direction. From jazzing up TV shows with snazzy intros to helping businesses look their best, I've done it all – and loved every minute.

When it comes to getting things done, I'm like a Swiss Army knife with a paintbrush. Need a brand identity that pops? I'm on it. Looking for marketing materials that make people go "Wow"? Consider it handled. Motion graphics that hypnotize viewers? That's my jam. Whatever the project, I'll roll up my sleeves and make magic happen.

Art isn't just my job; it's my life's passion. When I'm not creating for clients, you might find me tinkering with my camera or diving into digital painting. It's all about capturing those fleeting moments and emotions that make life beautiful – or sometimes just make people smile.

As a leader, I believe in empowering others and helping them shine. I take pride in fostering an environment where everyone feels heard and valued, while also encouraging them to reach their full potential.

When I'm not immersed in design, you'll find me taking a relaxed approach to life. I enjoy exploring topics like behavioral psychology, catching up on the latest movies, and dabbling in metallurgy—because who doesn't want to turn scrap metal into art? And yes, I'm a fan of the occasional sauna session followed by a brisk cold plunge.

I invite you to take a look around my portfolio and hope you find something that resonates with you. If you'd like to connect, feel free to drop me a line at wolfie@mikaelcedergren.com .

Landscape

Landscape photography holds a special place in my heart. It has a way of reminding me of my insignificance in the grand scheme of things. It puts into perspective how trivial our problems can be and shows me that I am but a small part of a greater whole. What captivates me even more is capturing the beauty that often goes unnoticed by others. It's about immortalizing moments that most people simply pass by without a second glance.

Kids

Kids are unpredictable, and that creates a welcomed challenge in photographing them. Anything can happen, and I love how they contribute to the shoot simply by being who they are.

Wedding

Wedding is not my focus, but it is has been an exquisit opportunity to show my style, and I happened to be featured in several wedding blogs - a humbling, yet flattering, experience for sure.

Candid Portraits

Photography is a genuine passion of mine, especially when it comes to capturing portraits. I love how a single moment can reflect someone's true essence, frozen in time by the photographer's skillful anticipation. I particularly enjoy the spontaneity of candid shots and staying agile on my feet to capture those authentic moments.

Interior

This ended up as a six page spread in one of the largest interior design magazines in Sweden - Plaza Interiör.

Music

Commissioned work to extremely talented music artists. Along with the job came a substantial friendship and I'm proud to know all of them.

Sports

This was a one time thing photographing a boxing match. And to be completely honest, I was bored. There was no drama, nothing of interest, but suddenly one of the matches went intense - I grabbed my camera and started shooting and thoroughly enjoy every bit of it.

Watches

Everyone that knows me knows that I'm crazy about watches. I'm not that into still photography, I prefer people, but my passion for horology fuels these shoots.
